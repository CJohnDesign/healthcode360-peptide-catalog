## What is Lathmized NAD+?

Lathmized NAD+ is a stabilized form of NAD+ (nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide) designed for improved bioavailability and shelf life compared to standard NAD+ formulations. NAD+ is a coenzyme essential for cellular energy production, DNA repair, and metabolic function.

## Administration

### Route

- **Sublingual** — Hold under the tongue for the time specified on the product label. Do not swallow immediately; allow absorption through the oral mucosa.
- **Oral** — Some formulations may be taken orally with water. Follow product-specific instructions.

### Timing

- Many users take NAD+ in the **morning** or before physical or cognitive demand.
- Avoid taking late in the evening if it affects sleep.
- Follow your provider's recommendation for your goals.

### Dosing

- Dosing is product-specific. Refer to the HC360 LNAD product label and your provider's guidance.
- Start at the recommended dose; do not exceed without provider approval.

---

## Storage

- Store in a **cool, dry place** away from direct sunlight.
- Some formulations may require **refrigeration** — check the product label.
- Keep the container tightly closed.
- Do not use after the expiration date.

---

## Cycling

Some protocols use intermittent dosing (e.g., 5 days on / 2 days off) to support cellular response and avoid tolerance. Your provider will tailor the protocol to your goals.

---

## Precautions

- Inform your provider of all medications and supplements you take.
- NAD+ may interact with certain medications. Consult your provider before use.
- Not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Use under provider supervision.
